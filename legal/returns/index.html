In general, we accept returns for most products we sell. However, we may not accept returns where:

  • Our 2-year Limited Warranty
  • Your statutory rights under UK consumer law

In the event of a confirmed fault, we may offer:

  • Replacement of the defective component (e.g., a single fan or cable)
  • Replacement of the full bundle where multiple parts are affected
  • A refund where repair or replacement is not possible

8. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you by email whether your refund has been approved.

9.2 Faulty, damaged, or incorrect items

If your item is faulty, damaged in transit, or we have sent the wrong product, we will normally provide a pre-paid return label or reimburse reasonable return shipping costs.

10. Exchanges

In many cases, we will treat an exchange as a return plus a new order. For example:

Where appropriate, especially for faulty items, we may offer a like-for-like replacement instead of requiring a new order.

11. Your Consumer Rights

Nothing in this policy affects your legal rights. If you are in the UK, you have statutory rights under consumer protection law, including the right to a refund, repair, or replacement if a product is faulty, not as described, or not fit for purpose.
