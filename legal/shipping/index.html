Depending on the shipping method used, your order may include tracking. Where tracking is available, we will normally provide a tracking number or link in your dispatch confirmation email.

8. Failed Delivery & Returns to Sender

If a delivery cannot be completed due to an incorrect address, repeated missed delivery attempts, or failure to collect from a local depot, the parcel may be returned to us.

In these cases, we can:

  • Re-send the order to a corrected address (additional shipping charges may apply)
  • Offer a refund minus the original shipping cost, if appropriate

9. Lost or Delayed Shipments

If you believe your order is lost or significantly delayed, please contact us with your order details. We will:

  • Check the status with the courier
  • Begin an investigation if necessary
  • Offer a replacement or refund where a parcel is confirmed lost

10. Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives with visible damage to the packaging or product, please:

  • Take clear photos of the damage
  • Retain all packaging materials
  • Contact us as soon as possible with your order number and photos

We will work with you to arrange a replacement, refund, or other suitable resolution in line with our Returns & Refund Policy and your consumer rights.
