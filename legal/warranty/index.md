    <h1>2-Year Limited Warranty</h1>
    <p>Last updated: 25 November 2025</p>

    <h2>1. Warranty Coverage</h2>
    <p>
      BonnieByte PC products are covered by a 2-year limited warranty against manufacturing defects
      starting from the date of purchase. This warranty applies to all fans, hubs, and accessories
      sold under the BonnieByte PC brand.
    </p>

    <h2>2. What’s Covered</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Manufacturing defects</li>
      <li>Faulty LEDs or lighting control issues</li>
      <li>Motor defects or electrical failures</li>
      <li>Hub/controller malfunction</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>3. What’s Not Covered</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Damage caused by incorrect installation</li>
      <li>Damage from liquid, modding, or short circuits</li>
      <li>Physical damage, drops, or user-caused wear and tear</li>
      <li>Products used outside intended PC applications</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>4. Replacements</h2>
    <p>
      If your product is found to be defective, we will provide a replacement free of charge.
      In some cases, we may request photos or a short video to help diagnose the issue.
    </p>

    <h2>5. Return Shipping</h2>
    <p>
      For most cases, we do not require customers to return the defective unit. If a return is needed,
      we will provide instructions and support.
    </p>

    <h2>6. How to Claim Warranty</h2>
    <p>
      To start a warranty claim, email us at:
      <a href="mailto:warranty@bonniebytepc.com">warranty@bonniebytepc.com</a>
    </p>

    <h2>7. Proof of Purchase</h2>
    <p>
      A valid proof of purchase is required. This may include an order confirmation from Amazon, eBay,
      our website, or other authorised sales channels.
    </p>

    <h2>8. Additional Support</h2>
    <p>
      If you’re unsure whether your issue is covered, contact support:
      <a href="mailto:support@bonniebytepc.com">support@bonniebytepc.com</a>
    </p>
