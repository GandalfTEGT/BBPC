4. What’s Not Covered?

This warranty does not apply to issues caused by:

  • Accidental damage (drops, impacts, liquid, crushed cables)
  • Incorrect installation or forcing connector pins
  • Connecting ARGB to a 12V RGB header
  • Short circuits, exposed wires, electrical misuse
  • Normal wear, dust build-up, or cosmetic changes
  • Small LED brightness variances or slight colour blending
  • Minor motor hum at low RPM (normal PWM behaviour)
  • Fan ramping caused by BIOS curves (normal behaviour)
  • Unauthorised repairs or modifications
  • Damage caused by fire, storms, floods, or similar events
  • Products sold as used, refurbished, or “for parts”
