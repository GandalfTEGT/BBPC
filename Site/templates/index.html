Our Story

Bonnie Byte PC was founded by a PC enthusiast, for PC enthusiasts. Frustrated by the choice between overpriced brands and questionable generic parts, we decided to build a better alternative.

The name 'Bonnie' comes from the Scottish word for beautiful. It reflects our commitment to not just performance, but beautiful design and craftsmanship. Our mission is simple: to deliver Beautiful Tech, from Scotland.
